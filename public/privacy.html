Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 18, 2026 • Valid for Credentia Platform

At Credentia, we prioritize your security and adhere to the highest standards of data protection. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our cybersecurity education platform.

1. Information We Collect

We believe in data minimization. We only collect the information absolutely necessary to provide a personalized educational experience:

  • Account Data: Name, email address, and authentication credentials.
  • Educational Metrics: Learning progress, quiz scores, earned badges, and platform interactions.
  • Technical Data: Basic device information and secure session tokens.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is processed strictly for the following purposes:

  • To securely authenticate you and maintain your session.
  • To track your mastery of topics and issue certifications.
  • To provide contextual AI Mentorship safely.

We do not sell your data to data brokers, advertising networks, or any third parties.

3. AI Mentorship & Data Processing

Our AI Mentor utilizes advanced language models. By design, any sensitive or personal information you accidentally input into the AI chat is isolated. However, we advise against pasting real-world credentials, proprietary code, or authentic PII into any simulated input field on the platform.

4. Security Protocols

We implement enterprise-grade security protocols to protect your data. This includes encryption at rest, secure TLS protocols in transit, strict access controls, and zero-trust architecture principles across our backend infrastructure.

5. Your Rights

You own your data. You possess the right to request a full export of your learning data, or request complete cryptographic deletion of your account and associated records directly from your in-app Settings panel.

6. Contact the Security Team

For privacy inquiries or vulnerability disclosures, please reach out to our security administration through the platform's contact mechanisms.
